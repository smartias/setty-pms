▾ ℹ️ How RFIs & Submittals work here — what to log, where, and when

1️⃣An RFI/submittal email landsLog or respond right from the Outlook add-in on that email — that's the day-to-day home for entering them, since you're already in the message.
2️⃣It came through Procore / FormaThose arrive as notification emails. Come here, pick the job, and 📥 Import the new ones in bulk — no re-typing. (Coming soon: these sync in on their own.)
3️⃣You need the whole pictureThis Tracker is the project-wide view — every RFI/submittal on a job, filter & search by type/discipline/status, and + Add / edit by hand when you're not in Outlook (a phoned-in RFI, cleanup, a one-off).
Only jobs marked "In Construction Administration" appear (⚡ = has Procore/Forma email filed). Put your name in the "You" box (top-right) so the log shows who added or changed each item. Safe by design: it never overwrites someone else's edit — it asks you to refresh instead.
