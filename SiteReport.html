Site Reports — what it does
  • Add photos from the project's existing field-photo gallery, or take new ones on your phone/tablet.
  • New photos are auto-saved to the project's Photos library on SharePoint, so they show up alongside Field Photos captures and don't have to be uploaded twice.
  • Tag items by discipline and location, and place pins on uploaded floor plans to create a photo key plan — with direction arrows showing where each photo was taken from.
  • Generate a client-ready Word report with one click — automatically saved into the project's SharePoint folder under 04 🏗 Site Field Reports, organized by visit date.
